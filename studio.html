Privacy by design

Patient photos stay where they belong.

Studio was built with one non-negotiable: patient images never leave the clinic's control. Everything runs locally in your browser. No server, no storage, no exceptions.

For clinics handling sensitive before-and-after photography, that boundary matters.

🔒

No upload, ever

Photos are processed entirely in your browser using local computation. They are never sent to SkinDay's servers or any third party.

🗄

No storage

SkinDay does not store, cache, or retain any images. When you close the browser tab, everything is gone.

✔

No account required

Studio is free and open. You do not need to sign in or create an account to use it.
