How it works

In the room, during the consult.

Visualize is designed for the consultation moment — fast enough to use in real time, accurate enough to be clinically credible.

01

Take a photo

A clean frontal or three-quarter photo taken during the consult. Standard clinic lighting works fine.

02

Select treatment

Choose biostimulation or filler, then select the treatment area and volume. The simulation parameters are set by clinical logic, not sliders.

03

Generate preview

The AI generates a realistic simulation in seconds. The photo is sent to the AI provider for this request only and is not stored or retained.

04

Review with patient

Walk through the preview together. The simulation carries a clear consultation-only frame so expectations stay grounded.

Data & privacy

What happens to the photo.

Visualize requires sending a photo to an AI provider to generate the simulation. That is a different data posture than Studio, and we are explicit about it.

The photo is used for that single request. It is not stored by SkinDay, not retained by the AI provider for training, and not linked to any patient record.

↗

Sent to AI for this request only

The photo is transmitted to an AI provider to generate the simulation. This is the only purpose for which it is used.

🗑

Not stored by SkinDay

SkinDay does not retain the photo after the simulation is generated. It is not saved to any SkinDay database.

❌

Not used to train any model

The AI provider processes the image for inference only. It is not used to retrain or fine-tune any model.

📋

Clinic's consent responsibility

Obtaining appropriate patient consent before generating or sharing any simulation remains the clinic's responsibility.
