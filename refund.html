Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy applies to credit purchases made for SkinDay Visualize. SkinDay Studio is free and involves no purchases.

Automatic refunds

Credits are automatically refunded if a simulation fails to generate, is blocked by a content moderation check, or returns an error. Refunds happen immediately and credits are returned to your balance. You do not need to contact us for these cases.

Credits never expire

Unused credits do not expire. There is no time pressure to use purchased credits, and no subscription to cancel.

Requesting a refund

If you believe you were charged in error, or if you experienced a technical issue that was not automatically resolved, contact us within 30 days of the purchase:

  • Email: hello@skinday.com
  • Include your account email and a brief description of the issue

We review all requests and respond within 2 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method via Stripe.

What is not refundable

  • Credits that have been successfully used to generate simulations
  • Purchases where simulations were generated and delivered as expected
  • Requests made more than 30 days after the original purchase date

Contact

For any questions about billing or refunds: hello@skinday.com

退款政策

最後更新：2026 年 6 月

本政策適用於 SkinDay Visualize 的點數購買。SkinDay Studio 免費使用，不涉及任何購買。

自動退款

如果模擬生成失敗、被內容審核系統攔截，或返回錯誤，點數將自動退還。退款立即生效，點數返還至您的帳戶餘額。這些情況無需聯絡我們。

點數永久有效

未使用的點數不會過期。購買的點數無使用時限，也無需取消任何訂閱。

申請退款

如果您認為被錯誤收費，或遇到未自動解決的技術問題，請在購買後 30 天內聯絡我們：

  • 電子郵件：hello@skinday.com
  • 請提供您的帳號電子郵件及問題的簡短描述

我們審核所有申請，並在 2 個工作天內回覆。退款透過 Stripe 退回至原始付款方式。

不予退款的情況

  • 已成功用於生成模擬效果的點數
  • 模擬效果已按預期生成並交付的購買
  • 原始購買日期 30 天後提出的申請

聯絡方式

如對帳務或退款有任何疑問：hello@skinday.com
