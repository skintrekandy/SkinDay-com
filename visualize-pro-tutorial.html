The three tabs

Everything lives behind three tabs at the top of your portal.

1

Studio  Build the before and after

Drop in your photos, pick a layout, and Studio aligns and crops them into a clean multi-angle grid. Everything is processed in your browser, so the photos never leave the room until you choose to save.

2

Visualize  Show where a treatment may lead

Pick a treatment and a few details to preview a likely direction during the consultation. It is a conversation tool, not a promise of results, and it draws on your own saved cases to stay grounded in what your clinic actually produces.

3

Case Library  Keep it, publish it, reuse it

Saved cases collect here. Publish the ones you like and they appear on your own website automatically through one line your developer installs once. With patient permission, the same case teaches your Visualize.

Make it yours first. Open Settings and add your clinic logo. The portal, your gallery, and every before and after you export take on your logo and brand colour on their own. Nothing says SkinDay to your patients.

Your team

Invite people from Settings. Everyone on your team uses all three tabs, Studio, Visualize, and Case Library. Roles are about who runs the account, not what each person can open, and every action is recorded against the person who took it, so nobody should share a login.

OwnerSigns the agreements and holds billing. Usually set up once, then hands the day to day to an admin.
AdminRuns the team: invites people, sets roles, and removes access. The person actually in the portal each day.
Injector / TechnicianA working seat for the people performing treatments. Full access to every tab.
ConsultantA working seat for the people running consultations. Full access to every tab.
MarketingA working seat for whoever curates the gallery and website. Full access to every tab.
StaffA general working seat for front-desk and support. Full access to every tab.
